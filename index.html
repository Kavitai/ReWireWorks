The core problem | Is the business leaking value?

Performance and growth does not depend on strategy, sales, or market opportunity. It depends on whether the business can convert ambition, investment and effort into value.

The leak occurs in the layer that is not visible. These are deeper operating conditions: decision rights, trust, information flow, alignment, friction, and accountability. When leaders do not understand the system conditions underneath they may end up solving the visible symptom while the underlying operating condition stays intact.

The result: Opportunities are sighted, strategy executed, effort and investment expended. Visible issues addressed. But Results fall short. Problems keep resurfacing in different forms. The pressure to stay productive creates short-term fixes. This erodes the very capability they need for long-term performance.

What ReWireWorks does | A system-level diagnosis to understand what is really happening.

ReWireWorks looks underneath visible problems. It helps leaders diagnose the operating conditions that influence conversion of ambition.

The business can stop funding isolated fixes and start choosing on better intervention points that are most likely to improve performance, protect margin, and support sustainable revenue growth.

Without the diagnosis, leaders can end up playing whack-a-mole: fixing what is loudest, funding interventions that do not hold, and trying to protect long-term capability while short-term pressure keeps pulling attention back to the next urgent issue.

The origin story | Built from inside out

ReWireWorks was created by Kavita Khanna from her experience of more than 25 years of working inside organisations across New Zealand, Australia, and India.

The diagnostic grew from questions used in real organisational settings to help leaders see what was really slowing performance, trust, alignment, and execution. Over time, those questions became a repeatable way to identify where organisational energy was being lost and where intervention was most likely to matter.

ReWireWorks now turns that insider diagnostic practice into a structured product: easier to access, easier to apply, and easier to track over time.
