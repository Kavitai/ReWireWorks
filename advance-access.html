Advance Access

Advance Access for businesses ready to diagnose what is really slowing performance

Advance Access gives your business early use of the ReWireWorks diagnostic ahead of wider commercial release. Participating businesses use the diagnostic in a real organisational context, receive a diagnostic report with key findings and recommendations, and provide feedback that helps refine the product reporting, and implementation experience. In return, participating businesses receive preferential early-access pricing, closer configuration support, and early influence over how the diagnostic develops.

Who Advance Access is for | What participation involves

Advance Access is for businesses at an inflection point: growing, restructuring, scaling, shifting strategy, or seeing performance slow despite continued investment.

If your organisation is a SME (Small to medium enterprise, with a workforce of between 50 and 300 FTE (Full time employees), and a management team between 3 and 8, you are an ideal candidate for advance access. Participation is targeted, not enterprise-wide. ReWireWorks uses a structured questionnaire with selected participants across key touchpoints in the organisation, including relevant internal stakeholders and, where appropriate, client or customer-facing perspectives.

You will need to fill out the advance access form. We will be in touch with you to have a no-obligation meeting where we will understand your business, work through the process, answer any questions you may have. From there, if you are interested, we will cost out the deployment and support you through the process.

How confidentiality and data are handled

ReWireWorks is designed with confidentiality and responsible data handling in mind.

Participation, reporting, and data use are structured to protect individual input and focus on organisational patterns, not personal attribution.

Data handling is aligned with recognised privacy, data protection, data sovereignty, and security expectations, including principles reflected in GDPR, SOC, and related standards. Specific arrangements are confirmed with each participating organisation before the diagnostic begins.
