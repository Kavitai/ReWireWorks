Maldives Connect

Conference Connection

Building bridges between organizational development practice and the unique context of the Maldives — a nation navigating rapid transformation while preserving its cultural identity and environmental stewardship.

The Maldives represents a fascinating case study in adaptive capacity: small, agile, globally connected, yet deeply rooted. These are the same tensions every modern organisation faces.

Topics Explored

  • Adaptive capacity in small, resource-constrained systems
  • Cultural preservation vs. modernization
  • Sustainability as a leadership imperative
  • Tourism industry transformation
  • Youth leadership and digital leapfrogging
