The ReWireWorks Dashboard

Once we have worked through the diagnostic in depth, we will also identify the signals or system indicators, that matter most in your environment and shape a dashboard that reflects your organisation's real leverage points. Designed with international standards (like ISO TC 260) in mind, these system indicators offer a sharper, governance-aligned lens on performance and shows whether your culture and leadership systems are building coherence or drag.

Here are just a few examples of what can be measured:

🔍

Trust in Escalation

Your system's capacity to detect and respond to friction, pressure or failure. Critical for risk, feedback, and resilience.

⚡

Energy-to-Impact Ratio

Whether human effort is translated into meaningful progress, or wasted through confusion or overwork. Essential for spotting performance leaks and misalignment.

💪

Contribution Confidence

A measure of how strongly people believe their strengths are recognised and used. If confidence is low, capability is under-leveraged and performance lags. Critical for understanding performance potential.

🎯

Alignment Clarity

A system-level test of how clearly strategy is translated into day-to-day action. Fuzzy direction leads to wasted movement and weak execution. Indicates effectiveness of strategy alignment.

🔄

Decision Latitude Index

Measures how much autonomy people have to make decisions in their role. Signals how effective or brittle your decision-making architecture is.

📡

Strain Signal Awareness

A test of whether your system can hear the early warning signs, before performance cracks. Crucial for adaptability, sustainability, and resilience.

🗣️

Voice Representation Score

Tracks who gets to shape what happens, and whose views consistently matter. Inclusion isn't a virtue signal; it is a pressure test for whether your culture matches your ambition.
