What to Expect

You've got challenges. Execution of strategy falling short of expected results, performance not shifting, teams not clicking, or transformation fatigue. But what you don't have is time to host consultants on-site or the bandwidth to interpret lengthy reports. That's where ReWireWorks is different. I don't drop in with a templated playbook nor will you have to get into a workshop spiral. I work virtually, consult flexibly. From diagnostic to delivery, everything is designed for clarity, speed, and traction.

Getting started

1️⃣

Step 1: Initial Call (Free)

A 30-minute virtual conversation to understand what's going on, what's at stake, and whether we're the right partner for your challenge.

2️⃣

Step 2: Preliminary Diagnostic (Free)

While the full ReWireWorks diagnostic is currently available through Advance Access, all new engagements begin with a brief online taster of the ReWireWorks experience. You will complete a quick diagnostic, which will surface preliminary system-level friction points across people, culture, or leadership practices that hinder your strategy's success.

3️⃣

Step 3: Your Gameplan

If we go ahead, I'll deliver a clear, punchy 1-page gameplan — no jargon, no fluff. This outlines the strategic actions we'll take, your key roles, and the measurable outcomes we'll deliver. For a limited time, you can become part of the advance access programme.

4️⃣

Step 4: Delivery partner

Should you want more help, I work alongside you to rewire the conditions that create the problem in the first place. I start by helping you get sharp on the real problem, surface the hidden assumptions and map what's actually going on, not just what's being said. From there, the people closest to the challenge and those with untapped perspective get invited to be part of developing solutions. This approach leverages your team's knowledge, turning internal insights into transformative action. The most effective solutions already exist inside your system. My job is to help you find them, shape them, and make them usable. This isn't change from the outside. It's transformation from within.

Engagement Models

Fixed Scope Project

We agree on the brief, timeframe, and outcomes. Everything is scoped upfront. Ideal for focused shifts.

Retained Partnership

If you want ongoing access for thought partnership, sense-making, and adaptive coaching, we set up an ongoing advisory partnership. You get a trusted advisor on call, without the need for a full-time hire. This model allows for flexibility as business needs evolve.
